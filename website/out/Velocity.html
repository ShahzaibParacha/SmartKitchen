Velocity 🏇🏻


Looking at our velocity, it can be seen that we started a little controlled in Iteration 1. We moved gradually and made sure we commit as much as we can deliver. Knowing what we knew after Iteration 1, we were much better equipped and therefore committed to doing more in Iteration 2 and Iteration 3. Iteration 2 took us a little longer than anticipated cause we had to implement the database, so we did that as our main priority and moved some low-priority issues to later iterations. In iteration 3, we were able to complete our app early on to the point where we wanted it to be when we planned our app back in Iteration 0. We then spent some extra time looking for smells and on the website.
